## Screen intent

### Document Owner workspace

Designed to support:

- Intake initiation
- Conflict-check review
- Drafting and redline handling
- Stakeholder review coordination
- Owner attestation
- Implementation and awareness planning
- Maintenance actions such as annual review, off-cycle change, deferment, exception, and retirement

### Document Owner QC assistant

Designed for the narrower case where the owner remains inside LogicGate and only needs a pre-submission quality checkpoint before governance review.

### Governance Team workspace

Designed to support:

- Intake triage
- Queue-based governance operations
- QC checklist execution
- Approval routing by tier
- Publication readiness and record retention
- Review calendar management
- Exception, deferment, retirement, and archival workflows

## Notes

- These are low-fidelity on purpose and avoid brand styling.
- The layouts assume LogicGate is the system of record for governance metadata and lifecycle states.
- Subagent touchpoints are represented as panels, prompts, summaries, and activity logs rather than final UI behavior.
